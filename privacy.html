Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

The Short Version

Willow respects your privacy completely. Your thoughts are yours alone. We collect nothing, track nothing, and send nothing over the internet.

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your Mac in:

~/Library/Application Support/Willow/

Your thoughts never leave your computer. There are no cloud syncs, no backups to external servers, and no data transmission of any kind.

Network Requests

Willow makes zero network requests. The app works entirely offline and does not connect to the internet for any reason.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not collect any analytics, usage data, or personal information. There are no tracking pixels, no telemetry, and no crash reports sent anywhere.

Third-Party Services

Willow does not integrate with any third-party services. Your data stays on your device and is never shared with anyone.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, feel free to reach out.
